Just Do It Or Lose It

- Tyriq Riggins Web Developer


Those who say they can and those who say they can't are both usually right. Beating the odds and remaining humble allows one to perfect their craft. Before being a web developer I was just like your average 19 year old dreaming more than I was exceling. In and out of school working long shifts as a dishwasher in the midtown of NYC. Eventually before I got comfortable and settled I've decided to take the initiative and make a quick career change in a field I've never imagined doing. Hey I'm on my phone everyday so I might as well create applications that'll allow other individuals to visit my finished work and hopefully one day that'll inspire them just like how major names in tech like Mark Zuckerberg and twin brothers Donte Morris and Dominic Morris inspired me. Both brothers Donte & Dominic created the app HoopMaps starred on hit show Sharktank, Zuckerberg on the otherhand obviously creating the worlds most used application Facebook & his networth is allegedly $77.8b. I'm currently taking studies at NYCDA a tech school in lower Manhattan and so far i'm so in love with it i'm taking my steps slow but i'm getting more excited everyday as I continue to learn more and more each day. Years from now the sky is the limit only the man upstairs knows what will continue but what I know is at the end of pain is success if you want to be the best you have to work the hardest regardless.
