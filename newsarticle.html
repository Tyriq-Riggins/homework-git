Former CEO of JIGGY RIGGY CO Transitions to a Software Engineer?

written by journalist Tony Adamo www.CNN.com

Tyriq Riggins on WVMR radio station along side underground rapper Kay Anthony and the interview crew.


Tyriq Riggins 20 year old account executive formerly ran a campaign of his designed merchandise "Jiggy Riggy" now enters a new field of studies in his career path. Just months before Mr. Riggins was a dishwasher in Midtown, NYC began to continue his dreams of programming web applications and eventually becoming a software engineer.

Mr Riggins is now studying at New york city code + design academy to pursue his web designing certificate. Aswell as attaining his position as an account executive at Jam Factory in Midtown, NYC. After the program he's looking to take his studies to C4Q a 10 month designed school for software engineers no upfront tuition so best of luck to the young man we wish the best for you!
